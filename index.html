Hi everyone, I am an MSc. Robotic Systems Engineering student at RWTH Aachen University currently working as a research assistant at Mechatronics and Mobile Propulsion Institute. My research interests lie at the intersection of Navigation, Autonomous mobility and Reinforcement Learning for Robots.

I completed my undergraduate degree at BITS Pilani where I majored in Mechanical Engineering.

I also had the opportunity to work at Mercedes-Benz R&D where I performed Topography Optimization to reduce Springback in the sheet-metal parts. Prior to this, I interned at a Waste management robotics startup which used Computer Vision and robotics to perform dry waste segregation.

I am enthusiastic about Autonomous Driving and eagerly waiting for the SAE Level 5 autonomous mobility in the streets! I also admire the Boston Dynamics Humanoids performing complex maneuvres. I also love cycling, going out for long walks and playing badminton during my free time.

Feel free to check out my CV and drop me an e-mail if you want to chat with me!
