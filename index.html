Ashwin Chakravartula

I am an MSc student in Robotic Systems Engineering at RWTH Aachen University, specializing in control, simulation, and learning-based methods for robotic systems. My work centers on environment perception, navigation, and trajectory generation, with a strong emphasis on deploying robust solutions from simulation to real-world platforms. Currently, I am a working student at the German Aerospace Center (DLR), where I develop surface reconstruction and offline trajectory planning pipelines for industrial manipulators.
